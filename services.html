Software

LHP Tech is a studio offering a full range of custom software development services for a wide variety of verticals and business domains.

Whether you are a start-up or an established business, we will be happy to assist you at any and every stage of the software development life cycle: from conceptualization, business analysis and prototyping to the development and final deployment.

Web & Mobile

Our web and mobile development teams have successfully completed over 200 projects for a variety of industries and domains, including e-Commerce, e-Learning, Analytics, Finance, Entertainment and more.

Through the years, we have perfected our delivery processes and workflows to cope with constantly changing requirements and tight deadlines that are the hallmark of complex web development projects.

Big Data & Analytics

We are here to help you make better decisions. Our Big Data Analytics products will help you analyze large amounts of data and gain useful insights. Whether you are looking to utilize data generated by your operations, or you want to leverage Machine Learning and A.I. technologies to improve customer experience we have a solution that fits your needs.

Our Big Data experts have completed projects for the telecom, finance and retail industries and are here to create custom solutions to help you improve customer retention, increase revenue and optimize internal data flow.

Cloud Engineering

Are you ready to change the way your businesses thinks about IT resources? Cloud technologies are the best way to reduce operational costs, improve productivity and help your business scale.

Whether you are looking to adopt Cloud technologies or migrate between cloud providers, we are here to do all the hard work for you. Woried about security in the cloud? Our expert team will create backup and disaster recovery plans for you and prepare you for ISO & SOC auditing.

Blockchain Technologies

We are constantly working to improve business operations using cutting-edge technologies. Our Blockchain products will change the way you handle and share documents and contracts.

From document management platforms to online transactional systems, we utilize Blockchain technologies to safeguard your data, reduce audit times and operational overhead and instantly handle dispute resolution.

Startup Engineering

Whether you have an idea and you want it to become a product, or you have lauched a product and are looking to scale up, LHP's engineering and strategy teams are here to help you.

We feel at home with issues like redesigning a product, executing a relaunch or simply optimizing for scale. If you've got the technical bits under control, our strategy and business team is here to help you with matters like funding, due diligence, business plans and marketing strategies.
